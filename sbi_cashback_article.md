# SBI Cashback Credit Card: The Uncomplicated Workhorse

Most credit cards make you work for your rewards. You track reward points, memorize multiplier categories, hunt for partner discounts, and figure out how to redeem points without losing value. 

The SBI Cashback Credit Card does exactly one thing, but it does it better than almost any card on the market: it gives you 5% straight cashback on almost every online purchase. 

No points to convert. No brand restrictions. The cashback is automatically credited to your statement the next month.

## What you actually get

| Spend Category | Cashback Rate | Monthly Cap |
|----------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Any Online Spend** (Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy, Myntra, etc.) | 5% | ₹5,000 |
| **Offline Spend** (In-store, POS swipes) | 1% | None |
| **Utility, Rent, Fuel, Wallets, EMI** | 0% | N/A |

*Note: The ₹5,000 monthly cap means you earn 5% on your first ₹1 Lakh of online spending every month. Anything beyond ₹1 Lakh earns 1%.*

## Where it loses

The SBI Cashback card is a pure spend-and-save product. It strips away the "lifestyle" benefits to fund the massive 5% cashback rate.

1. **No Airport Lounges:** As of 2023, SBI removed the 4 complimentary domestic airport lounge visits. It has zero travel perks.
2. **Exclusions apply:** You get 0% on rent, wallet loads, school/education fees, utilities, and fuel. 
3. **No Milestone bonuses:** Aside from the fee waiver at ₹2 Lakh, there are no extra bonuses for hitting spend targets.

## The Verdict

If your monthly credit card bill is mostly made up of Amazon, Flipkart, Swiggy, Zomato, Myntra, MakeMyTrip, and other online merchants, **this is the only card you need**. 

At just ₹20,000 of monthly spend, it puts nearly ₹10,000 of real cash back into your pocket every year, completely free of cost.
